CP1406/CP5638 - Assignment 1 - Project Plan

Name: AKRAM KASOZI

Goal

  • Increase the number of online bookings for both preventative and emergency services by 30% within the first six months of launch.

Success Evaluation

  • Track the number of online bookings.
  • Have a page and email that clients can provide some feedback on.
  • Measure the conversion rate, which indicates the percentage of visitors who complete their required actions, such as booking an appointment or signing up for a newsletter.
  • Analyze metrics that measure the time clients spend on particular pages and the bounce rate to ensure they find information useful and engaging.

Target Audience

  • Local pet owners, including families who want care for their pets.
  • Retirees with companion animals, as these pets play a significant role in providing compassion and companionship.
  • Young professionals and couples who often have pets and will benefit from the services provided.
